Here's all about Kakuma

Kakuma was formed in the year 1992 with displacement community of south Sudan due to heavy war in southern part. The main reception area was loki before the formation of Kakuma residential area. Today Kakuma is made up of more than 250,000Refugees living in the camp, the following are the nationalities living in the camp. Southese Sudan, Somalian,Sudanese, Burundian, Nigerian, congolese, ruwandese, Ethiopian, and ugandan.
